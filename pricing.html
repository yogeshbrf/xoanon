FAQ

Common
Questions.

Do credits roll over each month?+
Credits reset at the start of each billing cycle and do not roll over. On Architect, you can purchase top-up credits any time before renewal.
Can I switch plans at any time?+
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.
Is there a free trial on paid plans?+
Architect includes a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. You're only billed if you continue after the trial.
What payment methods do you accept?+
All major credit and debit cards via Stripe. Yearly Oracle plans are also available via invoice.
Do you offer student or nonprofit pricing?+
Yes. 40% discount for verified students and registered nonprofits. Reach out with proof of eligibility.
Can I use XOANON for commercial projects?+
All paid plans include full commercial usage rights for all generated content. Acolyte is limited to personal use.
